HealthKit iOS

Overview

HealthKit allows applications to connect to health and fitness data on iPhones and Apple Watches. In order to use this data, users must give permission to the app to communicate with the HealthKit store. In accessing this data, apps have the ability to read, share, or update the data stored in this central health storage system.
